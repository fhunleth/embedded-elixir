Roundup of Embedded Elixir Talks at ElixirConf 2017

 Posted on September 11, 2017  |  Frank Hunleth

ElixirConf 2017 was fantastic with a good selection of Nerves and embedded Elixir talks. Here’s a list:

  • ElixirConf 2017 Keynote - Justin Schneck
  • Elixir Native UI - Boyd Multerer
  • Building an Artificial Pancreas with Elixir and Nerves - Tim Mecklem
  • Embedded Elixir for Monitoring the Built Environment - Christopher Coté
  • Keep an Eye on the Sky with Nerves and Phoenix - Jeff Smith
  • Implmenting a MQTT client in Elixir - Martin Gausby
  • Realtime Vehicle Tracking with Elixir and Phoenix - James Smith

Have we missed Nerves and embedded Elixir presentations at other conferences or meetups? Please let us know in the comments.
